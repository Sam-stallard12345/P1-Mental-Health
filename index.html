Contact and Support In an Emergency


The Crisis Text Line provides free, 24/7 support for those in crisis. Text with a trained Crisis Counselor to receive immediate help and support.

741 741Text "Home"


The National Suicide Prevention Lifeline offers free and confidential support for people in distress, prevention and crisis resources for you or your loved ones, and best practices for professionals.

Samaritans is a confidential emotional support service for anyone in the UK and Ireland. They are available 24 hours a day to provide support for people who are experiencing feelings of distress or despair.

Mental Health Conditions


Depression is a common and serious medical illness that negatively affects how you feel, the way you think and how you act. Fortunately, it is also treatable. Depression causes feelings of sadness and/or a loss of interest in activities once enjoyed.

Anxiety disorders are the most common mental health concern in the United States. They are characterized by excessive fear or anxiety that is difficult to control and negatively impacts daily life.

Bipolar disorder is a mental health condition that causes extreme mood swings that include emotional highs (mania or hypomania) and lows (depression). These mood swings can affect sleep, energy, activity, judgment, behavior, and the ability to think clearly.

PTSD is a mental health condition that's triggered by a terrifying event — either experiencing it or witnessing it. Symptoms may include flashbacks, nightmares, and severe anxiety, as well as uncontrollable thoughts about the event.

OCD is a common, chronic, and long-lasting disorder in which a person has uncontrollable, reoccurring thoughts (obsessions) and/or behaviors (compulsions) that they feel the urge to repeat over and over.

ClearSpace is a platform that helps you to clear your head space, boosting mood and providing support with mental health.

How can I get support in an emergency?

In an emergency, you can contact the Crisis Text Line, National Suicide Prevention Lifeline, or Samaritans for immediate support.

What resources are available on ClearSpace?

ClearSpace offers resources on meditation, exercise, healthy eating, mindfulness, and more to support your mental health.
